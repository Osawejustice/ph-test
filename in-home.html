Preeminent Hands Healthcare offers a wide range of care services, including Special Needs Child Care, Companionship Care, Assisted Living Service, In-Home / Community Care, Day Program, and Hospice Care. These services are designed to meet various needs, from children with special requirements to adults requiring assistance with daily living activities and end-of-life care.

Yes, Preeminent Hands Healthcare offers comprehensive in-home care services. Their In-Home / Community Care program provides personal care, companionship, medication management, meal preparation, and other assistance in the comfort of your own home. They offer both short-term and long-term care options, with 24-hour service available if needed.

The Day Program is designed for adults who need care during daytime hours, particularly those living with family members who work or need regular breaks from caregiving. It offers a stimulating environment with social activities, cognitive exercises, physical activities, meals, and personal care assistance.

Preeminent Hands Healthcare provides Hospice Care for individuals with terminal illnesses. This service focuses on comfort and support, offering pain and symptom management, emotional and spiritual support, personal care assistance, and coordination with healthcare professionals. The goal is to create a peaceful environment for individuals in their final days, whether at home or in a care facility.
